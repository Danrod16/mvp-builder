Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the MVP building process.

How long does it take to build an MVP?

Depending on the package you choose and complexity of your idea, I typically deliver MVPs in 2-4 weeks. The Starter package takes about 2 weeks, Growth takes 3 weeks, and Scale takes around 4 weeks. This timeline includes planning, building, and testing.

What if I have no technical background?

That's exactly who this service is for! You don't need any technical knowledge - just a clear idea of what problem you're solving and for whom. I'll handle all the technical aspects from architecture to deployment, explaining everything in non-technical terms.

What happens after my MVP is built?

After delivery, you'll receive access to the deployed application and its codebase. I also provide a handover session where I'll walk you through how everything works. If you need ongoing support or additional features, we can discuss a maintenance plan or future development sprints.

What technologies do you use?

I typically build MVPs using modern, scalable tech stacks that include React, Next.js, Node.js, and various databases depending on your needs (MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Firebase). The specific technologies are chosen based on your project requirements and future scalability needs.

What's your payment structure?

I require a 50% deposit to start work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion and before the final handover. This ensures we're both committed to the project while minimizing your risk.

How many revision rounds are included?

All packages include two rounds of revisions after the initial delivery. This allows you to test the MVP and request changes based on your testing. Additional revision rounds can be arranged at an hourly rate if needed.
