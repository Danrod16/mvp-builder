Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our new service packages.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline varies based on the package you select and the complexity of your project. Our Basic package typically takes 3 weeks, the Advanced package takes 5 weeks, and the Premium package can take up to 7 weeks. This includes all phases from planning to testing.

Do I need any technical skills?

No technical skills are required. Our service is designed for clients without a technical background. We will guide you through the process and handle all technical aspects, ensuring you understand each step in simple terms.

What support is available after project completion?

Upon completion, you will receive full access to your project and a comprehensive walkthrough. I am happy to offer a fixed monthly subscription for unlimited development requests at $1200/month (Subject to terms and conditions). Additionally, every app is built with technologies that are easy to find developers for, using Tailwind for the front end and Ruby for the backend. I use my own boilerplate, which has more than 200 developers who would be happy to jump in and participate in freelance missions. I am also happy to recommend some other developers.

Which technologies are used in your packages?

We utilize a range of modern technologies tailored to your project needs, including Tailwind for the front end and Ruby for the backend. These technologies are chosen for their ease of use and the availability of developers. Our boilerplate is supported by a community of over 200 developers ready to assist in freelance missions.

How is the payment structured?

We require a 40% deposit to initiate the project, with the remaining 60% due upon completion and before the final handover. This structure ensures mutual commitment and minimizes risk.

Are revisions included in the packages?

Each package includes up to three rounds of revisions post-delivery, allowing you to refine the project based on feedback. Additional revisions can be arranged at an hourly rate if necessary.

Why is there no meetings or calls after the first discovery call?

After the initial discovery call, all communication is handled asynchronously through Trello boards. This approach allows me to focus all my time on development, keeping prices low. Clients can track the progress of each feature and provide feedback from day one. Asynchronous communication also ensures that project advancements continue smoothly, even across different time zones.
